About Me

Hi there :D! I am C. Croy-Perrett, a third year Computer Science Student at UCSC! In my free time, I enjoy coding games and making digital art. My favorite pastimes are creating builds in Minecraft, and honing my hand eye cooridination skills in the platformer Celeste. My favorite memories come from playing the collaborative role-playing game Dungeons and Dragons, having served as both player and dungeon master. There is nothing more beautiful than coming together with some friends to create a story for you all to share. I'm excited to be a part of art 101, so I can learn even more about how to use technology to expand my artistic horizons!
